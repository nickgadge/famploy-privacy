What Data We Collect

🔒 Famploy Copilot is local-first. Your profile data is stored on your own device using Chrome's built-in storage. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.

When you set up your profile in Famploy Copilot, you provide:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Personal Info Name, email, phone, location Autofill job application forms
Professional Info Current title, experience, skills, bio, CTC Generate cover letters, autofill fields
Online Profiles LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio URLs Autofill link fields on job forms
Resume PDF file (base64 encoded) Resume upload autofill, keyword matching
Application History Job title, company, URL, date applied Track your job applications locally

We do not collect: passwords, payment information, browsing history unrelated to job pages, or any sensitive personal data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2

How We Use Your Data

  • Autofill: Your profile data is used to fill job application form fields automatically on supported platforms.
  • Cover Letter Generation: Your name, skills, bio, and the job description are sent to our backend to generate an AI-powered cover letter using OpenAI's GPT-3.5 model.
  • Resume Match Score: Your skills and resume text are compared locally against the job description — no data is sent to any server for this feature.
  • Application Tracking: Job application details are saved locally on your device and optionally synced to our Supabase database to enable cross-device access.
  • Profile Sync: Basic profile information (name, email, skills, links) is synced to our backend database to enable future features like dashboard access.
ℹ️ Cover letter generation sends your profile + job description to our server. This data is used only to generate your cover letter and is not stored permanently by our AI provider (OpenAI).
3

Data Storage

Data Where Stored Who Can Access
Full profile (with resume) Your device (Chrome local storage) Only you, on your device
Basic profile (no resume) Supabase database (cloud) Famploy team only
Application history Your device + Supabase Only you
Cover letter AI requests Not stored (transient) OpenAI processes, does not store

Your resume file is stored only on your local device and is never uploaded to any server. It is converted to text locally for keyword matching purposes only.

5

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the extension's onboarding and profile screens.
  • Delete locally: Click "Reset All Data" in the extension popup to delete all locally stored data immediately.
  • Delete from cloud: Email us at privacy@famploy.com to request deletion of your data from our Supabase database.
  • Export: Your profile data is accessible via Chrome's developer tools at any time.
  • Opt out of sync: The extension works fully offline — cloud sync only happens when you save your profile.
6

Data Security

  • All communication between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Your data in Supabase is protected by row-level security (RLS) policies.
  • We do not store API keys, passwords, or payment information.
  • The extension does not inject scripts from external servers — all code runs locally.
  • We follow industry-standard security practices and regularly review our infrastructure.
🛡️ If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at security@famploy.com. We take all reports seriously.
7

Children's Privacy

Famploy Copilot is designed for job seekers and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
